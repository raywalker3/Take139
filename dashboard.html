Before you re-pair

A pastoral check before you change partners.

Re-pairing with someone new ends your current Couples bond on this platform and archives the report you generated together. We allow it because real lives change — spouses, counselors, mentors — if that change came with pain, we are sorry.

If you continue, your previous partner will receive a quiet, pastoral note letting them know the pairing has been archived. Nothing of theirs is deleted.

Safeguards in place:
  • A 30-day cooldown between re-pairs
  • A lifetime cap of 5 free re-pairs per account
  • The new partner must already have a paid, completed profile
