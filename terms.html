Terms of Service

These are the rules of the road for using Take 139 and IMAGO. We've tried to keep them human and short. By taking an assessment, buying a code, or signing into a dashboard, you are agreeing to what's below.

Section One

What Take 139 and IMAGO are — and what they're not

Take 139 and IMAGO are pastoral diagnostic tools. They are designed to help individuals and couples name patterns in their inner life and their relationship that may otherwise go unnamed. They are written by a senior pastor with seminary training in theology and counseling, and they draw on attachment theory, family systems work, biblical counseling, and a Christian theological frame.

They are not clinical assessments. They are not psychiatric diagnoses. They are not therapy. They are not a substitute for working with a licensed mental-health professional, a pastoral counselor, or a physician if you are facing serious distress. If you are in crisis — suicidal, in danger, or unsafe — please call 988 (in the US) or your local emergency number and contact a qualified professional. Take 139 cannot help with that, and was never designed to.

Plain language

If reading your report makes something hard surface, please bring it to a counselor, a pastor, or a trusted friend. The report can be a starting place for that conversation. It is not the whole conversation.

Section Two

Who can use the service

You must be at least 16 years old to take an assessment. By using Take 139 or IMAGO, you confirm that you are 16 or older and that the information you provide is your own.

Couples Reports are designed for two adults who have both chosen to participate. Do not submit an assessment on behalf of someone else without their consent.

Section Three

What you pay for, and what you get

Pricing is listed on the pricing page and at checkout. At the time these terms were last updated:

  • Single ($20) — one assessment, personal report, full walkthrough PDF
  • Couple ($40) — two assessments, two personal reports, two walkthroughs, one Couples Report
  • Connection Add-On ($10) — pair two existing single-tier accounts into a Couples Report

All prices are in US dollars. Payment is processed by Stripe. Once payment clears, your access code is delivered by email and is yours to use at any time.

Section Four

Refund policy

Take 139 and IMAGO are pastoral diagnostics. The report is delivered as soon as you complete the assessment — there is no way for us to "take back" what you've already read. For that reason, we do not offer general satisfaction-based refunds: the value of the report is in the reading of it, and a refund mechanism would invite the reader to dismiss what they found uncomfortable as a reason not to pay.

We do refund in these specific cases:

  • Technical failure. If the assessment crashes, the report fails to generate, or the email never arrives and we cannot recover it, we will refund in full.
  • Duplicate charge. If Stripe accidentally charges you twice for the same access code, the duplicate is refunded immediately.
  • Bought in error. If you purchase but never use the access code (no assessment submitted, no report generated), we will refund within 30 days of purchase on request.

To request a refund in any of these cases, write to hello@take139.com with your order details.

If reading your report surfaces something painful, that is the report doing what it was made to do. Please bring it to a counselor, pastor, or trusted friend. That is not a refund situation.

Section Five

Access codes, pair codes, and account ownership

When you buy, you receive an access code by email. Access codes are single-use for SINGLE and COUPLE tiers, and are tied to whoever first redeems them. CONNECT codes are single-use and bind one specific pair of profiles together.

Your dashboard account is tied to the email address you used at finalize. If you lose access to that email, write to hello@take139.com and we will help you recover it after verifying your identity.

You may not resell, redistribute, or share access codes commercially. They are licensed to you for personal pastoral use.

Section Six

Re-pairing and the orphan-notification email

If you choose to break a current Couples bond and pair with someone new from your dashboard, your previous partner will automatically receive a short, pastoral email letting them know the pairing has been archived. This is not punitive — it is a matter of basic honesty. We will not facilitate quiet, undisclosed re-pairings.

The first five re-pairs on each account are free. After that, additional re-pairs require a Connection Add-On ($10). A 30-day cooldown sits between successive re-pairs.
